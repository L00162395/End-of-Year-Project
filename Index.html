Why I started this website

My name is Philip, I have been playing guitar for over 20 years, I am based in Prosperous, Co. Kildare, I started this website as a way to pass on that knowledge, as I have been teaching my younger brother guitar for the past 5 years and have also helped my friends with the instrument over the years and I also have experience in teaching both kids and adults English in Asia and South America, so I combined the two together for this venture.


The area in which I can give lessons is North Kildare, if you live in an area close to this region I may be able to accomadate you, so please feel free to contact me here
